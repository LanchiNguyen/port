Brand Identity

Nhat Huong Bakery

Refreshing a legacy Vietnamese baking brand so its market leadership, ecosystem, and sense of inspiration are visible at first glance.

Project Info

ClientNhat Huong
RoleDesigner
Timeline6 months
ToolsPhotoshop, Illustrator
SkillsBranding, illustration, packaging
Summary

From inspiring taste to empowering bakers

I worked as part of a team to redesign Nhat Huong’s visual identity, enhancing how the brand communicates its legacy as a pioneer in the Vietnamese baking industry.

We focused on reflecting its comprehensive ecosystem, from production and education to distribution, while supporting its mission to spread the joy of baking and assert leadership in new markets.

The real challenge was not just modernizing the brand. It was translating decades of authority into a system that felt exportable, understandable, and emotionally resonant.
Challenge

Reshaping beyond baking excellence

Since its founding in 1998, Nhat Huong has become a symbol of success in the Vietnamese baking industry. But while the company had invested deeply in product excellence, it had spent less effort on brand building.

By digging into the company’s story, we saw an opportunity to reposition Nhat Huong as an inspirer: a brand rooted in Vietnam that empowers bakers and supports a wider baking community locally and internationally.

Approach

Made in Vietnam, for every kitchen

We used visuals as a strategic communication tool, not just an aesthetic layer. The refreshed system was designed to explain the brand’s ambition to bakers of different backgrounds, across retail, education, and distribution touchpoints.

Nhat Huong’s signature red remained central, expanded with a broader palette that could flex across applications. Typography balanced tradition and clarity: a strong serif for heritage, paired with a clean sans-serif for readability.

We also developed a graphic language that reflects the artistry of baking, creating repeatable patterns and shapes that could travel across packaging and promotional materials.

Outcome

A stronger system for products, services, and growth

The refreshed identity helped unify Nhat Huong’s product lines and services while making the brand feel more recognizable, approachable, and competitive against established international players.

Our aim was to make each product feel irresistible on shelf without losing the brand’s heritage. The resulting system gives Nhat Huong a clearer voice for future growth while still feeling unmistakably rooted in its original story.
