Brand Identity

Green Bite

A brand system for a sustainable meal service that needed more personality than the category’s usual clean-but-generic look.

Project Info

ClientPersonal project
RoleDesigner
Timeline1 month
ToolsPhotoshop, Illustrator, Figma
SkillsBranding, illustration, graphic design
Summary

Giving healthy food a more engaging visual voice

I designed the brand identity for Green Bite, a meal service focused on sustainability and healthy living. The concept emphasizes simplicity and natural energy through organic shapes, earthy colors, and modern typography.

My goal was to make the brand feel more vibrant and enjoyable than many meal-service competitors, which often come across as sterile or overly minimal.

Design Direction

Natural, playful, and grounded

I built a color palette around layered greens, bright accent tones like neon green and orange, and a deep brown that adds warmth and depth.

To make the system more flexible across touchpoints, I also created organic repeatable shapes that could unify packaging, product presentation, and communications without feeling rigid.
